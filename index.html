About me

After 10+ years in the arts, I was invited to deliver a keynote at PyCon UK 2023 about my work as a playwright and digital theatre-maker. I've always focused on the creative crossover between theatre and tech, staging ambitious stories about digital spaces, online worlds, and new ideas. But after that weekend surrounded by brilliant developers working across the industry, I started to think seriously about creating those digital spaces myself.

I have a background in events, producing, and digital communications. I'm particularly passionate about online accessibility.

I'm currently training with iO Academy, on their 12 week intensive Software Developer Essentials course, after completing their Coding Quick Start short course in 2024.
